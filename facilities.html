Campus & Facilities

A serene learning environment with state-of-the-art infrastructure on a lush green campus

Our Campus

A Sanctuary for
Learning & Growth

Spread across 7 acres of lush greenery, our campus is strategically located on Mundargi Road, near Adavi Somapur, away from the city's chaos. This peaceful environment provides the perfect setting for focused academic pursuits and personal development.

The campus features modern architecture blended with natural surroundings, creating an atmosphere conducive to learning, research, and holistic development. Every facility has been thoughtfully designed to support academic excellence and student well-being.

Infrastructure

World-Class Facilities

Smart Classrooms

Our spacious, well-lit classrooms are equipped with modern teaching aids, interactive projectors, and high-quality audio systems. We maintain a limited student-to-faculty ratio to ensure personalized attention and foster an engaging learning environment.

Advanced Computer Labs

State-of-the-art computer laboratories equipped with high-end workstations, the latest software packages, and dedicated high-speed internet. These labs provide the perfect environment for coding, software development, and specialized tech projects.

Central Library

A comprehensive collection of books, international journals, and digital resources covering every aspect of computer applications and technology. The library includes quiet reading zones and collaborative research areas for focused study.
