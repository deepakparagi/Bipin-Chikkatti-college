Our Heritage

Cultivating Minds,
Shaping Tech Futures

Established in 2019, Bipin Chikkatti Degree College is dedicated to providing transformative education in the field of Computer Applications. Affiliated with Karnatak University, Dharwad, and approved by AICTE, New Delhi, our institution embodies a commitment to accessible, quality education.

We believe in nurturing not just technical scholars, but well-rounded IT professionals equipped to navigate the complexities of the modern tech world while remaining grounded in timeless values of integrity, excellence, and service.
