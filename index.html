Founded in 1994 by Prof. S. Y. Chikkatti, Bipin Chikkatti College has been dedicated to providing transformative education that combines academic rigor with character development. Our institution embodies a commitment to accessible, quality education across a sprawling 7-acre lush green campus.

We believe in nurturing not just scholars, but well-rounded individuals equipped to navigate the complexities of the modern world while remaining grounded in timeless values of integrity, excellence, and service.

Visionary Leadership

Founded on a Vision
of Educational Excellence

In 1994, Prof. S. Y. Chikkatti established Bipin Chikkatti College with a singular vision: to create an institution that would democratize quality education and empower students from all backgrounds to achieve their full potential.

"Education is not merely about acquiring knowledge; it is about building character, fostering critical thinking, and preparing young minds to contribute meaningfully to society."

— Prof. S. Y. Chikkatti, Founder

Under his leadership, the institution has grown from a modest beginning to a comprehensive educational network, maintaining unwavering commitment to academic excellence, ethical values, and student-centered learning.

Our Journey

Three Decades of Excellence

Milestones that mark our commitment to educational excellence and institutional growth

1994

Foundation

Prof. S. Y. Chikkatti establishes Bipin Chikkatti College with a vision to provide accessible, quality education. The institution begins with Pre-University programs on a modest campus in Gadag.

2016

Expansion

Launch of Vinay Chikkatti PU College, expanding the institution's reach and introducing specialized Commerce stream programs. The campus infrastructure is significantly upgraded with modern facilities.

2020

University Affiliation

Establishment of degree college with affiliation to Karnatak University, Dharwad. Introduction of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Science, Commerce, and Management streams.

2024

30 Years Milestone

Celebrating three decades of educational excellence with over 5,000 successful alumni, 95% placement rate, and recognition as one of Gadag's premier educational institutions. The 7-acre campus now features world-class infrastructure and integrated coaching facilities.
