Campus & Facilities

A serene learning environment with state-of-the-art infrastructure on a lush green campus

Our Campus

A Sanctuary for
Learning & Growth

Spread across 7 acres of lush greenery, our campus is strategically located on Mundargi Road, near Adavi Somapur, away from the city's chaos. This peaceful environment provides the perfect setting for focused academic pursuits and personal development.

The campus features modern architecture blended with natural surroundings, creating an atmosphere conducive to learning, research, and holistic development. Every facility has been thoughtfully designed to support academic excellence and student well-being.

Infrastructure

World-Class Facilities

Smart Classrooms

Well-maintained, spacious classrooms equipped with modern teaching aids including projectors, audio systems, and comfortable seating arrangements to enhance the learning experience.

Advanced Laboratories

Fully furnished laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science, and Biology with latest equipment and safety measures, enabling hands-on practical learning.

Central Library

Extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources. Quiet reading areas and research sections provide an ideal environment for academic exploration.

Sports Complex

Comprehensive sports facilities including cricket ground, volleyball court, basketball court, and indoor games room promoting physical fitness and team spirit.

Computer Center

Modern computer labs with high-speed internet connectivity, latest software, and individual workstations for practical sessions and research work.

Cafeteria

Hygienic cafeteria serving nutritious meals and refreshments. A comfortable space for students to relax and socialize between classes.

Student Services

Transport & Accommodation

Transport System

Dedicated transport system connecting various parts of Gadag and surrounding areas, ensuring safe and convenient commute for students.

  • Multiple routes covering Gadag city
  • Well-maintained buses
  • Experienced drivers
  • Affordable transport fees

Hostel Facilities

Separate hostel facilities for male and female students with modern amenities and 24/7 security for a safe living environment.

  • Comfortable accommodation
  • Nutritious mess facilities
  • 24/7 security and supervision
  • Study rooms and common areas
Student Experience

Campus Life

Beyond academics, our campus fosters a vibrant community through cultural events, sports competitions, student clubs, and social activities. We maintain strict academic discipline while encouraging holistic development through regular student motivation programs and extracurricular activities.
