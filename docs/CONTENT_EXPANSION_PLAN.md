## CONTENT GUIDELINES

### Tone & Style:
- **Sophisticated** and academic
- **Professional** and trustworthy
- **Editorial** quality writing
- **Institutional** voice
- Avoid buzzwords and hype
- Focus on facts and achievements

### Length Guidelines:
- Home: 8-10 major sections
- About: 6-8 sections
- Courses: Detailed cards + 3-4 sections
- Admissions: 7-8 sections
- Faculty: 15-20 profiles + 3 sections
- Campus: 8-10 facility sections
- Placements: 6-7 sections
- Events: 12-15 news items + 4 sections
- Contact: 5-6 sections

### Visual Balance:
- Mix of text and images
- Card-based layouts
- Editorial grids
- Testimonial variations
- Timeline presentations
- Statistics highlights
