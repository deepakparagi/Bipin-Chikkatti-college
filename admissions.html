How to Apply

Admission Process

1

Application Submission

Complete the online application form or visit the college office to obtain a physical application. Submit all required documents including academic transcripts, identification proof, and passport-sized photographs.

2

Document Verification

Our admission committee will verify your submitted documents and eligibility. Original documents must be presented for verification during the final stage of admission.

3

Merit List Publication

Based on academic performance and eligibility criteria, merit lists will be published on the college website and notice board. Selected candidates will be notified via email and SMS.

4

Fee Payment & Enrollment

Selected candidates must complete fee payment within the specified deadline and complete the enrollment process to secure their admission.

Requirements

Eligibility Criteria

B.Sc. Programs

  • 10+2 with Science stream
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Mathematics/Biology as per program
  • Age: 17-25 years

B.Com. Programs

  • 10+2 from any recognized board
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks
  • Commerce background preferred
  • Age: 17-25 years

B.A. Programs

  • 10+2 from any recognized board
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks
  • No stream restrictions
  • Age: 17-25 years
Documentation

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • 10th and 12th mark sheets and certificates
  • Transfer certificate from previous institution
  • Migration certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Income certificate for scholarship
  • Aadhar card copy
  • Passport-sized photographs (6 copies)
