How to Apply

Admission Process

1

Application Submission

Complete the online application form or visit the college office to obtain a physical application. Submit all required documents including academic transcripts, identification proof, and passport-sized photographs.

2

Document Verification

Our admissions team will review your application and verify all submitted documents. You will be notified of any additional requirements or clarifications needed.

3

Merit List Publication

Based on academic performance and eligibility criteria, merit lists will be published on the college website and notice board. Selected candidates will be notified via email and SMS.

4

Fee Payment & Enrollment

Selected candidates must complete fee payment within the specified deadline and complete the enrollment process to secure their admission.

Requirements

Eligibility Criteria

B.Sc. Programs

  • 10+2 with Science stream
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Mathematics/Biology as per program
  • Age: 17-25 years

B.Com. Programs

  • 10+2 from any recognized board
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks
  • Commerce background preferred
  • Age: 17-25 years

B.A. Programs

  • 10+2 from any recognized board
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks
  • No stream restrictions
  • Age: 17-25 years
Documentation

Required Documents

Financial Support

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We believe financial constraints should never hinder academic aspirations

Merit Scholarship

Up to 50% fee waiver for students scoring above 90% in 12th standard examinations.

Need-Based Aid

Financial assistance for economically disadvantaged students based on family income.

Sports Scholarship

Special scholarships for students with exceptional achievements in sports.

Government Schemes

Assistance in applying for state and central government scholarship programs.
