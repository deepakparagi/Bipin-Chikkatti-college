How to Apply

A Simple, Transparent Admission Flow

Four structured steps keep the process calm, predictable, and easy to complete.

Step 01

Submit Application

Complete the form online or visit the college office with your basic academic and identity details.

Step 02

Verify Documents

The admissions team reviews marks cards, certificates, and category or scholarship records where applicable.

Step 03

Merit Review

Eligible applications are assessed as per university norms and admission committee guidance.

Step 04

Confirm Enrollment

Complete fee payment and final enrollment formalities within the communicated timeline.

Eligibility

Clear criteria for a focused BCA cohort.

The program welcomes students from Science, Commerce, and Arts streams who meet university eligibility norms and are ready to build a future in computing.

Academic Qualification

  • 10+2, PUC, or equivalent from a recognized board.
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks in qualifying examination.
  • 40% aggregate eligibility for reserved categories.

Eligible Streams

  • Science stream students may apply.
  • Commerce stream students may apply.
  • Arts stream students may apply as per university norms.

Admission Review

  • Applications are checked for completeness and eligibility.
  • Original documents are required during final verification.
  • Selections follow institutional and university guidelines.

Student Support

  • Guidance is available for application completion.
  • Scholarship-related documentation can be reviewed.
  • Office staff help clarify fee and enrollment timelines.
Documentation

Documents Required

Keep these ready before final verification to make your enrollment experience smooth.

Application Form

Completed and signed application form with accurate contact details.

Marks Cards

10th and 12th marks sheets and passing certificates.

Transfer Certificate

Original transfer certificate from the previous institution.

Migration Certificate

Required for students from applicable boards or universities.

Category Certificate

Caste or category certificate where reservation benefits apply.

Income Certificate

Needed for scholarship and financial support consideration.

Aadhar Card

Clear copy of the student's Aadhar card for identity records.

Photographs

Six recent passport-sized photographs for admission records.
